Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

TaskSync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TaskSync Calendar application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Information

When you connect your Google account, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if available)
  • Google Calendar access tokens (stored securely in session)

2.2 Task Information

We collect information about the tasks you create:

  • Task titles, descriptions, and schedules
  • Priority levels and categories
  • Calendar event details you choose to sync

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Create calendar events in your Google Calendar
  • Maintain your session while using the application
  • Display your recent task history (stored locally in your browser)
  • Improve our service functionality

4. Data Storage

Important: TaskSync uses session-based storage for authentication tokens. Your Google access tokens are:

  • Stored temporarily in server memory during your session
  • Never persisted to disk or database
  • Automatically cleared when you sign out or close your browser

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs to sync tasks with your Google Calendar. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your Google account at any time
  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove task history
  • Request information about your data

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@tasksync.app
